Kawaguchiko

Kawaguchiko offers a tranquil lakeside retreat with breathtaking views of Mount Fuji. Located about 2 hours from Tokyo by bus or train, it is ideal to visit in spring (April–May) for cherry blossoms or autumn (October–November) for crisp air and colorful scenery.
