Welcome to Scenic Tokyo!

This site lists the top 7 scenic locations near Tokyo for an unforgettable day trip.

Featured Locations

  • Hakone

    Hakone is known for its hot springs, stunning views of Mount Fuji, and relaxing lakeside activities.

  • Nikko

    Explore the UNESCO World Heritage-listed Toshogu Shrine and natural wonders like Kegon Falls.

  • Kamakura

    Home to the iconic Great Buddha and scenic beaches, Kamakura is perfect in spring when cherry blossoms bloom.

  • Shosenkyo Gorge

    Shosenkyo Gorge features dramatic cliffs and waterfalls, ideal for short hikes, especially during autumn foliage season.

  • Kawaguchiko

    Kawaguchiko offers stunning views of Mount Fuji and a peaceful escape near the lake.

  • Enoshima

    Enoshima is known for its scenic beaches and the famous Enoshima Shrine, perfect for a relaxing day trip.

  • Yokohama

    Yokohama is a vibrant city with impressive landmarks like the Minato Mirai and its beautiful waterfront views.
